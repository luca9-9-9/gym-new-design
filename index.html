About

The platform was created for new artists/producers. It will help them to sell instrumentals, mix master services, artworks, visualisers etc. or to link up with other musicians and create different sounds to fall in love with, while you gain fans, likes and views. Selling musical products/services will help you as a artist/producer to reach the top of the leaderboard and receiving annual gifts from Whogotbars, Pop Cola, Cipiraw, a contract from a record label or a chance to perform to a brand new festival. So keep scrolling to create an account or to log in and start grinding.
